World Food Production (1961-2013)

Food production is a critical and dynamic aspect of the global ecosystem, affecting not only our physical health, but also our economy, culture and environment. In a changing world, the world food production continues to evolve due to factors such as increasing population and people's growing demand for nutrition.
From 1961 to 2013, this dynamic visual map provides a concise overview of the changing patterns of global food production. By choosing different years, changes and trends in agricultural output over time can be observed.

Food Production by Category

Food production not only varies over time but also differs based on the type of food, and the rates of change also vary. This area chart displays the production of major food items in various countries around the world from 2000 to 2013.
By clicking on the different food categories listed in the legend on the right side, the corresponding area will be retained, while the opacity of the other areas will decrease.
